## 🎯 Quick Steps (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Repository (1 minute)

1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. **Name:** `uas-integra-costing-suite`
3. **Description:** "Professional costing tool for Integra UAS"
4. Select **Public**
5. Click **Create repository**

### Step 2: Upload All 6 Files (2 minutes)

1. Click **Add file** → **Upload files**
2. Drag & drop or select these 6 files:
   - `index.html`
   - `README.md`
   - `QUICK_START.md`
   - `DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md`
   - `LICENSE`
   - `.gitignore`
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 3: Enable GitHub Pages (1 minute)

1. Click **Settings** tab
2. Click **Pages** (left sidebar)
3. **Branch:** Select "main"
4. **Folder:** Select "/" (root)
5. Click **Save**

### Step 4: Wait & Access (1 minute)

1. Wait 2-3 minutes for deployment
2. You'll see: ✅ "Your site is published at..."
3. Click the link to access your live tool
